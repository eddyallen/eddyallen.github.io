About Eddy Allen

Solo artist, singer, songwriter, guitarist. Plays with a loop pedal for full band sound with one guitar.

Duo - Accompanied by either percussionist, saxophone, or trombone

Trio - Accompanied by percussionist and brass

Full band - Accompanied by percussionist, bass, saxophone, and trombone

Style: World fusion music. A versatile mix of Pop, Jazz, latin, Afro, gypsy.
